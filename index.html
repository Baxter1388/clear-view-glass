About Us

Clear View Glass Works is a professional auto glass service provider specializing in truck, RV, auto, flat glass, headlight restoration, rock chip repair, and GlassParency coatng. Our mission is to provide high-quality, reliable glass services to residents and businesses in Central Oregon. With our commitment to superior craftsmanship, customer service, and innovative glass solutions, we aim to become the go-to choice for auto glass needs in the region.
